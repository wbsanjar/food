Swaado is a community-driven food listing platform, born from a simple yet powerful thought — to give every home chef, baker, chocolate maker, and homemade snack maker a space to be seen, acknowledged, and supported. Behind every dish made in home kitchens is a story — of passion, courage, tradition, and dreams. We’re here to make sure those stories don’t stay hidden.

Whether it’s a mother reviving childhood recipes, a grandmother preparing sweets the traditional way, or a young baker experimenting with flavors — Swaado is an online listing platform that showcases the heart behind homemade food makers and helps them get discovered by people searching for homemade snacks near me or home chefs near me.

We’re not just a listing service. We are your voice, your support system, and your partner in building a meaningful home-based food business. From helping you list your food business online to making your presence felt in your local area, Swaado stands with every passionate food maker ready to take the next step.
